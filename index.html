Installation Guide

Follow the steps listed below to install ONEMONITAR on your target phone. Please note, the steps are universal for most Android phones, however if you don’t find the same settings, try similar options with different names.

Step 1. Before installing ONEMONITAR

1. Open Google Play & Click on Disable Play Protect & Click on icon

2. Turn off Scan apps with Play Protect
 

Step 2. ONEMONITAR Installation

Download the app by clicking on the above button (Download ) or direct by clicking this link and install the app after installing the app you will get icon as shown in next step (screenshot).
